AIM AND SCOPE


The aim of Journal of Integrated Dental Sciences & Education is to provide a genuine, transparent, unbiased and quality platform to the Dental Research to archive and disseminate their knowledge in various fields of dentistry and Dental Education

Journal of Integrated Dental Sciences & Education


Dentistry can easily be considered as one of the most dynamic streams of Health Care Sciences. It is a unique amalgamation of science, art and techniques. Journal of Integrated Dental Sciences & Education aims to play an ode to this beautiful specialty by archiving and disseminating the related knowledge as well as updating the same. It shall be published monthly in order for constant and frequent dissemination of knowledge, following stringent peer review protocols.

The journal shall be an educational kaleidoscope of research in various branches of Dentistry through carefully and transparently selected good quality manuscripts. Through this journal we also intend to focus on “Dental Education” and the related innovations as it is an integral part of Dentistry. We wish this journal to establish itself as a journal truly “By the Dentists, Of the Dentists and For the Dentists”.
